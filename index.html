What is smartmall magazine?

Smart Mall magazine is the very first quintessential shopping magazine in India. This one of a kind magazine is all about the latest trends in every aspects of shopping. From gadgets to jewellery, clothes to accessories, home decor to motor vehicles...Smart Mall presents you with the best your money could buy in each and every month. Know your malls via Smartmall. Mall shopping never got so much easier. After all this is Smartmall... the smartest way to mall shopping. And that’s not all...smart mall will also be the first in Kerala to promote its tourism. Smart Mall will strive hard to achieve precision and thereby winning the hearts of his readers.
