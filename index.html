What is smartmall magazine?

Smartmall magazine is India’s first comprehensive monthly shopping magazine specializing in the shopping arena. This magazine is a bandwagon that helps to meet the needs of the family members in Kerala. It lays its spectrum in the regional as well as international markets. Features the latest deals, informative details, services and everything that one is looking forward for.

Smartmall will move India’s shopping destinations and shopping festivals. Recreating the latest trends in the world of jewels, watches, beauty, culture, fashion and health centre’s. We also enlighten the tourism sector, travel and luxury hotels. Electronic devices and gadgets too get their prominence. We also promote special occasions through our magazine. A huddle-free, vivacious design and superior visual content, positions smart mall among the best looking magazine in the country. We are here to provide you everyday solution and promise to build a trustworthy relationship. It is a new kind of magazine for a new kind of reader, one who demands both style and substance.
